About

Sitting on her front porch swing, my grandmother taught me to knit and crochet at an early age. Working with yarn is so calming and I have enjoyed this hobby off and on my entire life. Now that I am retired I have more time to spend on it and with my stepdaughter having a baby girl any day I have been making baby items. Check them out in the yarnology section.

I have always been fascinated with photography and have combined it with my love of nature to capture the local flora and fauna here in East Tennessee. Some of my favorite shots are located in the photography section.
